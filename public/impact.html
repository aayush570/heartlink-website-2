NGO Jwala · Women’s empowerment

Visibility for women whose stories deserve a wider world.

Through leadership in Jwala, Gopi Shah has helped advance nationwide women’s empowerment and humanitarian initiatives. As programme coordinator for Brides of Tribes, she brought tribal women and their living heritage into view through fashion, craft and cultural storytelling.

The work reflects a principle that also shapes HeartLink: tradition has greatest dignity when the people carrying it possess agency, recognition and a meaningful voice.
