Co-Founder & Principal AdvisorGopi Shah
Led by experience and trust

Gopi Shah personally guides the HeartLink process.

Gopi Shah has worked across India and the United States in culture, community work and matrimonial service.

Her background includes Home Science from MS University of Baroda, creative direction, Indian craft work and cultural projects across Mumbai, Chicago and Florida.

She has coordinated major cultural work, including a performance at the Great Wall of China, and has received the Dr. Sarojini Naidu International Award.

Before HeartLink, she guided Jeevansathi - Juhu as an honorary matrimonial service for well-regarded Gujarati families. That work was personal, private and never treated like a public database.

Gopi is not an outside adviser studying these circles from a distance. She understands the schools, expectations, family concerns and social nuance her clients bring to the table because she has lived inside the same world.

Her role is central to HeartLink's process: helping good families move with more clarity when they are still learning how to understand each other.
