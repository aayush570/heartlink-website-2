Privacy

Your details stay private.

HeartLink collects only the information needed to review your enquiry. Identity is never displayed in a public directory.

How HeartLink protects your information

Privacy is a core part of how HeartLink works.

Application details are reviewed only for verification and matchmaking. Prospective introductions receive information only after approval. HeartLink does not sell applicant data or use it for public advertising profiles.

Applicants may request access, correction or deletion of submitted information by writing to privacy@heartlink.in ↗.
