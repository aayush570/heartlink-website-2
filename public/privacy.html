Privacy protocol

Discretion, made operational.

HeartLink collects only the information required to assess and serve a private mandate. Identity is never displayed in a public directory.

Our working principles

Private information is not inventory. It is entrusted context.

Application details are reviewed for admissions and verification purposes. Prospective introductions receive information only through controlled, consent-led disclosure. HeartLink does not sell applicant data or use it for public advertising profiles.

Applicants may request access, correction or deletion of submitted information by writing to privacy@heartlink.in ↗.
