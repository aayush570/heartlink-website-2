The methodology

Not a funnel. A seven-part architecture.

A private, living process that evolves as two people move from consideration to commitment, and as two families learn to move together.

Foundation · Selection
01

Personalised Partner Selection

We begin beyond the checklist. Personality, patterns, priorities, preferences, family dynamics and the life each person is actually building become the basis for a highly considered search.

Composition · Curation
02

Profile Presentation

Only carefully selected possibilities are presented. We consider mental, emotional, intellectual and social alignment, then offer the context required to see the person rather than merely scan a profile.

Observation · Reassessment
03

Compatibility Reassessment

Once two individuals connect, theory gives way to lived chemistry. We reassess organic compatibility, observing the ease, curiosity and priorities emerging between them.

Dialogue · Counsel
04

Courtship Counselling

Alpa creates a confidential space for honest conversation: how each person gives and receives care, handles difference, defines partnership and recognises whether their needs are truly being met.

Readiness · Clarity
05

Pre-Marital Counselling

Before celebration comes clarity. Bride and groom explore rights, responsibilities, emotional labour, personal ambition, finances, boundaries and the expectations that shape everyday married life.

Integration · Family
06

Family Guidance

We work thoughtfully with both families to create mutual respect, navigate the wedding process and establish the deeper understanding upon which long-term family integration depends.

Continuity · First year
07

Post-Marital Support

For up to one year, HeartLink remains close. We support the couple through personal, professional and social transitions, helping a new marriage find its own confident rhythm.

The constant throughout

One relationship. One continuous line of counsel.

You are never passed from sales to matching to support. Context is preserved, judgment compounds and confidentiality remains intact.
